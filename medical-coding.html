Overview

Accurate coding is where clean revenue begins

A single mis-keyed or outdated code can trigger a denial, an underpayment, or a compliance flag. Our certified coders assign precise ICD-10, CPT, and HCPCS codes based on your documentation, ensuring every billable service is captured and coded to current payer and federal guidelines.

We stay current with annual code updates and payer-specific rules, applying rigorous quality checks so your claims reflect the care delivered — no more, no less.
